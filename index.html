Explore IOE

The Institute Education (IOE) is a degree-granting, interdisciplinary academic entity of AUCA – dedicated to teaching, research and advocacy of education at all levels and in trans-cultural contexts. IOE is the institutional home of Master of Arts in Teaching (MAT) – Central Asia’s only dual-degree graduate program in teacher education, offering Master’s Degrees from Bard College, New York, and the Kyrgyz Ministry of Education. IOE strives to support the policies of the Kyrgyz Ministry of Education, and collaborates with scholars and practitioners globally to research innovative approaches to education for all children, adolescents and youth in Central Asia.

Research

Recent and on-going research initiatives include: higher education and youth perspectives on sustainable development in Kyrgyzstan, employability and role of soft-skills in Central Asian post-secondary context, politics of private-public partnerships in basic education, and implications of online-mentoring for pre and in-service teacher education programs.
